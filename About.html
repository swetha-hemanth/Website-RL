About Raja Lodge Ooty

Experience Comfort & Legacy Since 1980

Welcome to Raja Lodge Ooty, your trusted haven in the Queen of Hills. With a rich legacy spanning over 45 years, we’ve proudly served thousands of happy guests who return for our warmth, hospitality, and unbeatable location just 5 minutes from the bus stand and railway station.

Established in 1980, Raja Lodge has stood the test of time by constantly evolving while staying true to traditional Tamil hospitality. We offer a serene stay amidst the beauty of Ooty, providing clean, comfortable rooms, friendly service, and easy access to all major sightseeing destinations.

Whether you're a solo traveler, family, or group of friends, we ensure your comfort, safety, and convenience. With round-the-clock support, modern amenities, and the charm of a lodge that feels like home — we invite you to be part of our journey.

Thank you for trusting us for over four decades. At Raja Lodge, we don’t just offer rooms — we offer a memorable Ooty experience.
