About Raja Lodge, Ooty

Welcome to Raja Lodge – a trusted name in Ooty for over 45 years. We pride ourselves on delivering cozy, budget-friendly accommodations just a short walk from Ooty’s Bus Stand and Railway Station. Our guests enjoy a peaceful stay, warm hospitality, and easy access to all tourist spots.
