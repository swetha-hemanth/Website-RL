About Us

With over 45 years of heartfelt hospitality, Raja Lodge Ooty has welcomed travelers from across the globe to experience comfort, warmth, and local charm. Nestled just minutes from the Ooty bus stand and railway station, our family-run lodge offers a peaceful stay with easy access to the best of the Queen of Hills.
