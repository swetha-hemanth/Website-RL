Guest Guidelines

Kindly follow the rules to ensure a safe and peaceful stay 🌿

  • 🕐 Check-in: 12:00 PM | Check-out: 11:00 AM (Next day)
  • 🪪 Guests must present valid government photo ID during check-in. (Aadhar, PAN, Passport, Driving License)
  • 🚻 Only the number of guests mentioned during booking will be allowed inside the rooms.
  • 🔇 Kindly maintain silence especially during night hours (after 10 PM).
  • 🎶 Loud music, smoking inside rooms, or any type of party is strictly not allowed.
  • 🚫 Alcohol consumption, gambling, or illegal activities are not permitted on the premises.
  • 🚭 Smoking is strictly prohibited in the rooms. Use the designated smoking zones.
  • 🚪 Visitors are not allowed in rooms after 9 PM.
  • 🔥 No fire camp facility is available inside or around the lodge premises.
  • 🐾 Pets are not allowed unless special permission is granted in advance.
  • 💡 Please switch off fans, lights, geysers, and electrical appliances before leaving the room.
  • 🧹 Room cleaning and housekeeping available on request before 5 PM.
  • 🚯 Use dustbins provided in rooms and common areas. Help us keep the place clean.
  • 🪟 Avoid hanging clothes on balcony railings or windows.
  • 🍽️ Cooking is strictly prohibited in rooms. Kitchen access is restricted to long-stay guests with prior approval only.
  • 💰 Guests will be held responsible for any damage to property including linens, furniture, or appliances.
  • 🔐 Management is not responsible for any lost valuables. Please lock your rooms securely when leaving.
  • 💳 Full payment is to be made during check-in. We accept UPI, GPay, PhonePe, and cash.
  • 🛑 Any behavior causing disturbance to other guests may result in cancellation of stay without refund.
  • 📞 For any help, call the lodge number shared at check-in. We're happy to assist!

💖 Thank you for choosing Raja Lodge Ooty — We’re committed to making your stay relaxing, safe, and memorable.
